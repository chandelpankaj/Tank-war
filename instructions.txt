Works better with google chrome version 58.0 and above

Instructions to play:
Use 'w','a','s','d' or arrow keys to move
Space to shoot bullets
Enter key to pause/resume

a/d OR left/right arrow keys to change level/speed
Press esc any time to come back to main menu

If the player is blocked, i,e head is blocked and is not possible to
rotate in left/right direction then it can always move in backward
direction.
